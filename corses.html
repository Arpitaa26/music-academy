Hindustani Vocals for Beginner

This level is the next step in exploring the nuances of the genre by strengthening the basics and introducing a higher level to this curriculum thereby inculcating within the student a broader sense of the artform. The minimum age of the student should be five.

Desideratum

COA-CC1

Class Duration

40 - 50 Mins.

Classes

10 sessions (minimum)

In this level the following concepts will be covered:

  • Varnams
  • Introduction to new Ragams
  • Singing in different kalams/speed.
  • Brushing up the foundation lessons in various ragams.
  • ntroduction to the new concept of Raga Sancharam and Manodharmam

Hindustani Vocals for Advance

This level will delve deeper into the genre with primary focus on the improvisational aspect of the artform along with an introduction to complex compositions. This level will build a stronghold on the depth of the artform. The minimum age of the student should be ten.

Desideratum

COA-CC2

Class Duration

45 - 55 Mins.

Classes

10 sessions (minimum)

This level will be split into three modules. The following concepts will be covered at this level:

  • Introduction to Hindustani Music
  • Introduction to the basics including the concepts of Saptaswaras, Vowels, Aaroh, Avaroh.
  • Detailed exercises to build a strong foundation.
  • Introduction to Thaat
  • Understanding Swaras and Taal
  • Introduction to the concept of a Baandish
  • Introduction to Sargam Geet and Basic Taans
